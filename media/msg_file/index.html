For photographers

The Fashion Camera Book + Planner + Jewelry

I wrote a book called 'The Fashion Camera' to inspire all the starting fashion photographers! In this book I shared everything I learned in the past 10+ years. I'm not only sharing my secrets about the techniques and equipment but also about the business side of this job. The perfect guide for every photographer who want's to go pro! The (Dutch) book is sold by different high-end bookstores and webshops in The Netherlands and Belgium.

Besides the book, I also created a planner (sold out) and jewelry for photographers. These are only available in my own webshop.
